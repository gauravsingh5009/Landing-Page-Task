A Collective Experience

Born in Haridwar. Made for the Soul.

SOUL Prakriti is Haridwar’s only 100-acre farmhouse enclave, where ancient wisdom meets slow design. With just 150 farmhouses, Prakriti isn’t a colony — it’s a collective.

105 Curated Experiences.5 Zones of Soulful Living.

Here, your villa is built under mango trees. You sip tea under the stars. And you sleep to the sound of silence. Prakriti offers more than land — it offers 5 zones that are a tribute to intentional living. This farmhouse community is designed to inspire movement, mindfulness, and memory.
